Features of the Application

Evaluate
The Quiz feature of Gurbani Vocab allows you to evaluate yourself by asking you multiple choice questions based on the vocabulary list you selected. The instant results help you to immediately see the areas in which you need to improve, giving you the most efficient way to study!

Progress
Track your progress per vocabulary list under the Progress tab. Under your profile, download your results and send them to your teacher or just watch as your grades improve!
